Contact Info

I would love to hear your questions, comments, criticisms, creative ideas, and ideas for helping out. Don't be shy -- send your thoughts! Feedback from interesting people is essential to keeping any community project thriving and improving. Please try to be concise.

There are several places you can send feedback:

  • User mailing list: This is the place to send support requests (e.g. "How do I do X?" or "I think Y is broken; am I using it right?"). It's also a good place to discuss techniques and strategies for using Macker, and ideas for new functionality.
  • Developer mailing list: This list is for discussion of implementation issues among developers. While it's public and you're welcome to listen in, please don't post unless you are discussing implementation / release planning issues, or have a potential patch to offer.
  • Bug database: If you have something that you're pretty sure is a bug, you can submit it here. You can also attach a patch if you have one. If you're not sure whether a behavior is correct or not, or what you have in mind is really additional functionality, bring it up on the user list instead.
  • If you don't want to involve the whole mailing list, you're quite welcome to contact me (Paul) personally. I enjoy hearing from users, and although it sometimes takes several days, I do always try to follow up personally.
